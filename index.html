Staying Focused is Hard

I find that I sometimes get distracted when sitting at a computer. It's very easy to open a new tab and head over to reddit or imgur. Before long, a bunch of time is lost - time that would have been better spent on work or projects. I made this little app to increase my productivity while working at a computer. You can too! It's simple:

Just follow the timer.

Focus intently on your work for three-fourths of an hour, then reward yourself with fifteen minutes of downtime. Aim to spend atleast five minutes of that time out of your chair.

Made by Jeremy Atkinson, during my downtime.
