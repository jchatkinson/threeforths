Staying Focused is Hard

Let's face it - its really easy to procrastinate while working at a computer.

Three Fourths is a free app designed to increase your productivity while working at a computer. It's simple: Just follow the timer.

Focus intently on your work for three-fourths of an hour, then reward yourself with fifteen minutes of youtime. Aim to spend atleast five minutes of that time out of your chair.
